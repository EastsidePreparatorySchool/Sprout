Here's Our Recommendations!



1. Reflection


OK. Take 5 minutes and say any and every stressor that comes to mind. Which ones can you solve? Which ones CANT you solve? Start by focusing on the stressors you can



2. Cardio


Do cardio for 5 minutes. Set a timer on your phone or watch and go. Literally pushups, situps, jumping jacks, jump rope, just MOVE for 5 minutes.



3. Self-Care


Sleep. Eat. Hydrate. Move. Lacking one? Fill the gap. Putting your health first is ALWAYS the best idea.



4. Finding Help


With moderate stress, it can be useful to speak with a trusted individual in your life. Being vocal about stressors means your trusted community can help you out.
