Here's Our Recommendations!



1. Reflection


Think of a time you overcame self-doubt, nervousness, and anxiety in your life. Remember you've done this before, so you can do it again.



2. Cardio


Go on a quick run or just be outside to absorb the fresh air. If you can't go outside, try sitting next to the nearest window for some air.



3. Self-Care


Sleep. Eat. Hydrate. Move. Lacking one? Fill the gap. Putting your health first is ALWAYS the best idea.



4. Finding Help


Talking to friends and family is an excellent de-stressor.
