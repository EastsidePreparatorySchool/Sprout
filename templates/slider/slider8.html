Here's Our Recommendations!



1. Reflection


Grab a notepad. Write down every source of stress that is impacting you. Then, cross out the ones that are out of your control. Ex. what grade you will get on your history presentation or math test you had today. Then going top down on the list, identify how you can diminish the impact of each stressor. ex. stress about a math test -> study 15 minutes.



2. Cardio


Do cardio for 10 minutes. Set a timer on your phone or watch and go. Literally pushups, situps, jumping jacks, jump rope, just MOVE for 5 minutes.



3. Self-Care


Sleep. Eat. Hydrate. Move. Lacking one? Fill the gap. Putting your health first is ALWAYS the best idea.



4. Finding Help


Please reach out to the EPS Counselors if you consistently remain at this stress level. Just do it please.
