Here's Our Recommendations!



1. Reflection


We understand. Stress can make it difficult to concentrate and focus on any tasks at hand. So, grab a notebook or open up a blank page and write/type the TOP FIVE things you need to get done/focus on. For the next hour, you must limit yourself to thinking ONLY about those 5 things. Go outside and repeat this square breathing exercise 5-10 times. Inhale 5, hold 5 seconds, exhale 5 seconds. Then, do cardio for 5 minutes. Set a timer on your phone or watch and go. Literally pushups, situps, jumping jacks, jump rope, just MOVE for 5 minutes.

3. Self-Care


Sleep. Eat. Hydrate. Move. Lacking one? Fill the gap. Putting your health first is ALWAYS the best idea.



4. Finding Help


Please reach out to the EPS Counselors if you consistently remain at this stress level. Just do it please.
